Mission Statement


Our mission at Ramen House is to give our customers a chance to try real and authentic ramen. Our ingredients have been picked locally from businesses in the Bay Area and made in-store. Ramen House hopes to give it's customers a chance to try gourmet Japanese cuisine at the comfort of the Bay Area.
